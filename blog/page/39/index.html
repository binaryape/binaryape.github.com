New Ape?

by Binaryape - Aug 10th, 2003 - posted in Ancient Archives

Has a new species of great ape been discovered? Maybe. It looks to me as if it’s at most a new species of chimpanzee.

I’m deeply skeptical of the chimp-gorilla hybrid theory. It may or may not be physically or genetically possible, but I just can’t see chimpanzees or bonobos being socially compatible with gorillas. Although bonobos will have sex with anything, I don’t think that the notoriously chaste gorillas would put up with it.

Link: Mystery Ape

(Describing chimpanzees as carnivorous is a bit extreme - they can hunt and eat meat, but most of the diet is vegetable based, I think)

The Odeon Website Again

by Binaryape - Aug 3rd, 2003 - posted in Ancient Archives

I grumbled at length about the awful Odeon website in a blog post last year. It is possibly the worst commercial website I have ever seen.

Now someone has built a site that wraps up the official site content, and reshapes it to make it useful! Now you can access the Odeon site on something other than Internet Explorer for Windows. The unofficial site runs faster, is clearer, easier to use - apart from the lack of online ordering, it’s better in every way.

The Odeon should immediately pay Matthew Somerville for his effort, and then fire everyone responsible for the original site. Or lock them in a room with Jacob Nielsen until they repent.

Link: The Accessible Odeon
