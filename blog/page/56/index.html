Oolong the Pancake-hat Rabbit

by Binaryape - Oct 18th, 2002 - posted in Ancient Archives

Last year I stumbled upon a picture of a rabbit balancing a pancake on its head. The page was in Japanese, so I had no idea why the rabbit had a pancake on its head. The rabbit didn’t seem to mind.

Thanks to a link on Metafilter, I’ve just found a huge site dedicated to photos of the same rabbit, Oolong. His owner has the strange habit of balancing small objects on his head and then taking photos. I’m a bit disturbed by this one, but Oolong seems to live a happy, varied and adventurous life, very different to the lonely lives of most pet rabbits in Britain.

Link: Oolong the Rabbit
